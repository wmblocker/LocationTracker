How to use Location Tracker

Initializing the Device

  • When you first open the Location Tracker App, give the app access to your location. The Location Tracker will track your location every 3 min as long as the app is running.
  • Next, open and read the Terms and Conditions. You will not be able to use the app unless you agree to these terms
  • If you disagree, you have the option to go back to Terms and Conditions page and agree.

Signing In

  • To sign in to the Location Tracker app, Initialize the device first. After the app is initialized, Click the sign in with Google button.
  • Once you have successfully signed in, you will be directed to the profile page.

Requesting to watch a users location

  • Once you have initialized the device and successfully logged in, Navigate to the bottom of the profile page and click the request button
  • Enter an email that is registered to use the Location Tracker app. If the email is valid the request will be sent.
  • Once the requested user accepts the request, you will have permission to watch their location

View users requesting to watch your location

  • Once you have initialized the device and successfully logged in, Navigate to the bottom of the profile page and press the view request button
  • If you have any pending requests, they will display on this page.
  • You have the option to either accept or deny a request.
